Team 13TRIS

13TRIS is the Project we 5 developed for the lecture of Software engineering 1&2 at the during the course of two semesters.

13TRIS is meant to be a dummy project for us to develop, while at the same time playing the roles of the project management team. It's a great way to get a glimpse of how companies manage the development of their software and all the thoughts that go into resource planning. The goal was not to programm however we wanted, in a chaotic and thoughtless way, but to structure and lay out a plan / future steps for the development process.

To keep things organized we utilized the following services to help us maintain controll over everything: Blog post S3 W2

13TRIS is the world's (maybe not) first block placing game with a special twist !
13TRIS is the name of our spin-off from the classic game Tetris, but with some features and cool things added. One feature is that every 13th block is a special block with evil powers. the other feature is the multiplayer. Being able to play against other players is something the original Tetris didn't provide in this magnitude (we will ignore the existence of Tetris99 for the sake of this argument).
